Data & Privacy

Note: We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your data. Please read the following information carefully.
What Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to generate your Farmer ID card, such as your name, address, date of birth, and contact details. No sensitive personal data (like Aadhaar, PAN, or bank details) is required or stored. Uploaded photos are used solely for card generation and are not shared with third parties.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used exclusively to generate your digital Farmer ID card. We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or analytics. No data is sold or shared with any external parties.

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely on our servers in India. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or misuse. Uploaded photos and personal details are deleted from our servers after your card is generated.
