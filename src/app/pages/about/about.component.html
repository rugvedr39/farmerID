About Us

Disclaimer: We are not related to any government body or agriculture body. The card generated from our website is not an official card of Agristack.

Welcome to All Digital Services, a service dedicated to helping farmers create professional identity cards for general purposes.

Our platform allows farmers to create personalized ID cards that include their details in both English and Marathi, complete with QR code integration for easy verification.

We've designed our service to be simple, affordable, and accessible to all farmers across India. While our cards are not official government documents, they can be useful for general identification purposes.

You can include your official farmer Agristack ID on the card to make it more useful for your needs. Our goal is to provide a valuable tool that helps farmers present themselves professionally.

Stay tuned for more tools and template designs in the future as we continue to expand our offerings to better serve the farming community.
